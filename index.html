Why this exists

Good research shouldn't die in a drawer.

Too many honest projects — a B.E. or B.Sc. capstone, an MBA dissertation, a first real study — end up unread. Not because the work was weak, but because no one showed the researcher the path, and the tools were scattered across a hundred tabs, emails, and spreadsheets. Throughline is meant to be the guide that's actually there: it walks you from a question to something you can submit, and keep revising — on your own, or with a supervisor who finally has something concrete to react to. Free, because the point is to help.

The throughline

Eight steps. One thread.

Follow a project from first curiosity to submission. Watch what each step carries forward — that hand-off is the whole point.

Not vaporware

The hand-offs are real.

These aren't loose links between separate apps. The work is encoded and carried forward — pre-filling the next tool so you never start from a blank page.

🔍

Discover → Read

A rising term in Wordmap opens straight into PaperCards' citation network, seeded and mapped.

wordmap → #seed=<term> → papercards
📦

Read → Frame

Papers you've compared — their recurring constructs and the gap between them — carry into ResearchFlow's idea stage.

papercards → #discovery → researchflow
🧬

Design → Collect → Write

A ResearchPack threads your question, scales, and design through Cadence, ToolsScope, and JournalTime.

researchflow → #pack → cadence → toolsscope → journaltime

Two ways in

However you arrive, it meets you there.

Do the project

Working through a B.E. / B.Sc. / MBA project? Throughline keeps you on your university's guidelines as you move through the suite — and turns the work into a submission-ready paper you can keep revising, on your own or with your guide.

Run the research

Early-career or seasoned, you already know the drill — so offload the busywork. One calm workspace instead of a hundred emails, PDFs, and spreadsheets, with the thread held for you so your attention stays on the thinking.

An assistant at every stage

Not a wall of features to learn. A guided flow that meets you where you are, tells you what's next, and lends a hand at any step — discovery, design, analysis, or writing.

Why trust the output

Rigour, not just vibes.

And an honest word: great tools already exist — Research Rabbit, JASP, Qualtrics, and more. They're powerful, mostly separate, and several gate or charge you at the worst moment. Throughline doesn't try to out-feature them — it stitches the whole journey into one intuitive flow that guides you through, and stays free.

Real statistics

A hand-written stats engine — t-tests through mediation — verified against textbook values, with effect-size benchmarks and APA write-ups.

Validated measures

Scales carry their reliability, dimensions, and original citations. No improvising the questionnaire.

Theory with receipts

~400 theories grounded in key studies and applications, mapped by influence and shared constructs.

Private by default

Everything runs client-side in your browser. Your data and your participants' responses stay on your machine.
